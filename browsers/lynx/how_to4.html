[ xmlSE . how to . edit a record ] edit

To add more information to a record, or to make changes, go to the record, and follow the 'edit' link.

Enter your name and password when prompted.

You should see a series of fields containing the information that has been entered for the record so far.

When you are finished editing the record, hit the 'update' button on the bottom of the page. The changes you have made will immediately be written to the category xml file, but will not be reflected on the site until you rebuild the site.
